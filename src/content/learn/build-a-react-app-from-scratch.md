### How to upgrade my free account to paid {/*how-to-upgrade-my-free-account-to-paid*/}

<Intro>
You can upgrade your account from the QA Touch itself by clicking Upgrade Now in the header.
</Intro>

![upgrade image](../assets/faq-screenshots/upgrade/upgrade.png)
![QA Touch Subscription image](../assets/faq-screenshots/upgrade/QA-Touch-Subscription.png)

From there, click Pay with Card and complete the payment. Your account will be upgraded to the selected plan.

### Do you have QA Touch Annual subscription plan? How to purchase QA Touch Annual Plan? {/*do-you-have-qa-touch-annual-subscription-plan-how-to-purchase-qa-touch-annual-plan*/}

Yes. We have an annual subscription option for both the Startup and Professional plan and you can avail the same. You will get 20% Off when you subscribe on an annual basis.

### Do I have to sign a long term contract? {/*do-i-have-to-sign-a-long-term-contract*/}

No, there are no long-term contracts.You can pay on a month-by-month and if you cancel your account, you will never be charged again.

### When will be my billing cycle starts? {/*when-will-be-my-billing-cycle-starts*/}

Billing cycle starts on the date you purchased the plan and recurs on the same day of each month for a monthly plan. If you are in the annual subscription, it will recur in the same year next year.

### How can I update my payment details? {/*how-can-i-update-my-payment-details*/}

You can update your card details from Administration -> Subscription -> Update Card Detail tab.
