### Overview {/*overview*/}

Save time and money with timesheet tracking feature which has been introduced over QA Touch

A timesheet is a tool used to log hours worked by employees on a project or task.

### How to enable Timesheet category in QA Touch? {/*how-to-enable-timesheet-category-in-qa-touch*/}
1. Login to QA Touch
2. Click on any Project -> Timesheet
3. Click on the Add Timesheet. Overlay will be open click the "Get Editable Option" enable timesheet category

![geteditable image](../../assets/faq-screenshots/timesheet/geteditable.png)

### How to add Timesheet category in QA Touch? {/*how-to-add-timesheet-category-in-qa-touch*/}

Goto **Administration -> Custom Option -> Timesheet Category**

1. To customize the options Click on Get Editable Options in Custom Option page (Timesheet Categories Tab)

2. Now a copy of all the existing options of the field is available for you to customize

3. Click on Edit to Rename/ or disable the option

![QA-Touch-Custom-Options image](../../assets/faq-screenshots/timesheet/QA-Touch-Custom-Options.png)

### How do we add a timesheet in QA Touch? {/*how-do-we-add-a-timesheet-in-qa-touch*/}

![timesheet image](../../assets/faq-screenshots/timesheet/timesheet.png)

To add timeshet to QA Touch, follow these steps:

Login to QA Touch

1. Click on any Project -> Timesheet
2. Click on the Add Timesheet, on the top right side of the screen
3. Fill in the details and click Save.
